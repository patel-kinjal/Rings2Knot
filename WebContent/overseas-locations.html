Dubai

The jewel in the crown of the Middle East is the wonderful Dubai. With world class infrastructure, iconic skyscrapers, a dazzling skyline and stunning resorts, the city has positioned itself as one of the best tourist and wedding destination in the region. From the timeless tranquillity of the desert to the lively bustle of the souq, Dubai as a wedding destination offers a kaleidoscope of attractions for visitors

And since everyone we know could use a holiday of that variety, why not invite all your nearest and dearest to Bali and throw a wedding into the mix
