Ramada Alleppey

Location Alleppey, Kerala
Category Luxury Wedding
Your dream of a wedding in 'God's own Country' can be fulfilled by Ramada-Alleppey.Relish the soul of Alleppey at Ramada. Set amidst a sprawling 3 acres of refreshing greenery, facing the Punnamada backwaters, where the celebrated annual Nehru Trophy Boat Race is conducted, Ramada-Alleppey offers you the best of nature and uber luxury, making it the best destination to mix business with leisure.

Merging flawlessly with the backscapes, the 7 storied Ramada-Alleppey lets you experience the ultimate in hospitality. Located conveniently, Ramada-Alleppey is accessible through water, road and air. The roof-top helipad comes as a thoughtful addition.
