Itinerary

Day 1: Island of Oahu – Waikiki Beach: Aloha and welcome to Hawaii! Upon arrival at the Honolulu International Airport, you will be met with the traditional Hawaiian flower lei, assisted through baggage claim and transferred to your hotel located in the very center of Waikiki. The remainder of your day is at your leisure to explore this fascinating world-renowned city within a city. Overnight stay in Oahu.

Day 2: Oahu: Day of Leisure to orientate yourself in Hawaii. Overnight stay in Oahu.

Day 3: Oahu: Today you will be taken on a half day tour of the city of Honolulu and the Arizona Memorial, one of the highlights of the Island of Oahu. Evening at Leisure. Overnight stay in Oahu.

Day 4: Oahu – Kauai: This afternoon, you will have a flight into Lihue, Kauai, and the beautiful “Garden Isle”. Upon arrival, your transfer will be waiting to take you to your hotel. Overnight stay in Kauai.

Day 5: Island of Kauai: Your driver and guide will pick you up for a tour to the westernmost side of the island to the Waimea Canyon, the “Grand Canyon of the Pacific”, with views along the way of the Menehune Fishpond, Spouting Horn, Hanapepe Valley, and the Captain Cook’s Landing. This evening is at your leisure. Perhaps you would like to enjoy a Luau tonight! Overnight stay in Kauai.

Day 6: Kauai – Kona, Hawaii: Today mid-morning depart your hotel for the Lihue airport for a short flight to the city of Kona on the Big Island of Hawaii, with your awaiting transfer to your hotel. Rest of the day is at leisure. Overnight stay in Kona, Hawaii.

Day 7: Kona, Hawaii: Day free at leisure. Overnight stay in Kona, Hawaii.

Day 8: Hawaii: Today’s excursion is full day tour of the “Big Island” of Hawaii. Your driver and guide will be taking you through Kona coffee farms, macadamia nut orchards and to the Volcanoes National Park where you will see newly formed lava fields, eruption sites and learn how these islands got their beginning. A lunch stop will be made, usually right at the Kilauea Crater Rim. The evening is at leisure. Overnight stay in Kona, Hawaii.

Day 9: Kona, Hawaii – Maui: Have a leisurely morning in Kailua, Kona then it’s on to Maui. Upon arrival at the “Valley Isle’, your driver will take you alongside the scenic West Maui mountains enroute to the hotel where you will stay for two nights. The evening is at leisure. Overnight stay in Maui.

Day 10: Island of Maui: Depart your hotel early morning for a tour of Hana Adventure where driver guides add to the experience with fascinating stories and colorful narration about the breathtaking views along the winding coastal road to Hana. Spend the day viewing lush rainforests, magnificent waterfalls, and breathtaking views. Overnight stay in Maui.

Day 11: Maui: The tour ends today and your hosts will provide you with all the information needed for leaving the islands. Check-out and transfer to the airport for onwards journey.
