A peaceful retreat offering pure relaxation, The Westin Siray Bay Resort & Spa, Phuket is nestled on Siray Island off Phuket's east coast. Our resort boasts breathtaking ocean views and has its own calm secluded beach ideal for renewing the mind, body and soul. The sky is the limit with six restaurants and bars for guests to refuel, Heavenly Spa by Westin™ to rejuvenate, fun activities at the Westin® Kids Club, generous meeting facilities overlooking Siray Bay and a choice of three refreshing outdoor pools.
