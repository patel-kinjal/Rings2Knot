Dunsvirk Court

Location Mussoorie
Category Luxury Wedding
Dunsvirk Court is a Luxury boutique hotel sits on the top of the hill in Mussoorie and boasts spectacular views of the surrounding valley. It's spacious rooms each have panoramic mountain views which no photography can do justice to! If you're looking for a peaceful private destination surrounded by mountains and woods , then this hilltop resort in Mussoorie is the place for you. Dunsvirk Court - Mussoorie is an ideal location for Destination Weddings, Honeymoon, Exclusive Parties, High Level Conferences and Film Shoots.

Owned by Judge Dunsvirk during the British Raj, this hotel was then bought by the Baroda Family and used as their summer retreat. Retaining its traditional architecture, it has been recently renovated and restored with all the luxurious amenities that a modern day traveler looks for and more, as it is large enough to be a great location for large groups yet small enough to be perfect for an peaceful and intimate holiday.
