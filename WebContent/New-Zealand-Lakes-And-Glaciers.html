Itinerary

Day 1: Christchurch: On arrival in Christchurch, ‘The Garden City’ transfers to your hotel and check-in. Overnight in Christchurch.

Day 2: Christchurch – Glacier Region: Breakfast at the hotel. Depart Christchurch on the Tranz Alpine Train and travel over the Southern Alps and into Greymouth. Travel south along the rugged West Coast to arrive at the glacier region of Franz Josef and Fox Glaciers. Overnight in Glacier Region (B)

Day 3: Glacier Region: Breakfast at the hotel. A full day at leisure to explore this magnificent glacier region. Optional activities include a valley walk to the face of the glacier, a scenic flight or helicopter on to glacier for a guided walk on the ice. Overnight in Glacier Region (B)

Day 4: Glacier Region – Wanaka: After breakfast, travel down the West Coast past spectacular mountain ranges, native forests and lakes to the Haast Pass, gateway to the World Heritage Westland National Park. Continue around the shores of Lake Hawea to arrive in Wanaka early afternoon. Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight in Wanaka. (B)

Day 5: Wanaka-Queenstown: Breakfast at the hotel. This morning you visit Puzzling World, a unique attraction of puzzling eccentricity with its bizarre buildings, puzzle center and illusion rooms. Continue to Queenstown for overnight. (B)

Day 6: Queenstown: Breakfast at the hotel. A free day to explore the endless optional attractions available. Queenstown offers bungy jumping, jetboating, white water rafting, gondola rides and much more. Overnight in Queenstown. (B)

Day 7: Queenstown-Mt Cook: After breakfast, travel south through Central Otago to Twizel before traveling around Lake Pukaki and into Mt Cook National Park. You can enjoy optional activities at Mt Cook include scenic flights, a glacier boat ride and the Sir Edmund Hillary Center on your own. Overnight in Mt. Cook. (B)

Day 8: Mt Cook – Christchurch: Breakfast at the hotel. Depart Mt Cook and stop on the shores of Lake Tekapo to visit the ‘Church of the Good Shepherd’. Continue across the fertile Canterbury Plains to arrive in Christchurch for overnight. (B)

Day 9: Christchurch: Breakfast at the hotel. Your tour concludes today with a departure transfer to Airport for your flight to next destination. (B)
