Category Honeymoon

Itinerary

Day 1/ Mahe: Welcome to the beautiful land of Seychelles. Upon arrival at the airport you will be transfer to your selected hotel. Enjoy your rest of the day at leisure to explore this beautiful island. Overnight in Mahe.

Day 2/ Mahe: After breakfast, Optional: why not see a different view of Seychelles on a fabulous catamaran cruise around this breathtaking archipelago ? They’re perfect for the more adventurous cruiser, relax or you can hire a car and take a leisurely drive around the island. Possibility to tailor make your own itinerary within the reserved hours. Overnight in Mahe. (B)

Day 3/ Mahe: After breakfast, the day is at leisure to explore this beautiful island on your own. Overnight in Mahe. (B)

Day 4/ Mahe – Praslin: After breakfast, transfer to inter-island pier. Enjoy your ferry transfer from Mahe to Praslin jetty. On arrival at Praslin jetty you will be transferred to your hotel. Overnight in Praslin. (B)

Day 5/ Praslin: After breakfast, you can hire a car and take a leisurely drive around the island. Possibility to tailor make your own itinerary within the reserved hours. Overnight in Praslin. (B)

Day 6/ Praslin: After breakfast, the day is at leisure to explore this beautiful island on your own. Overnight in Paslin. (B)

Day 7/ Praslin: Today after breakfast, you can extend your stay to La Digue Island OR you will be transfer to inter-island pier. Enjoy your ferry transfer from Praslin to Mahe Jetty. On arrival at Mahe jetty you will be transferred to Mahe airport for your departure flight. (B)
