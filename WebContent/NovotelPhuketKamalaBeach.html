Novotel Phuket Kamala Beach

Location Phuket
Category Luxury Wedding
Novotel Phuket Kamala Beach is located on the west coast of Phuket on Kamala Beach, near Phuket Fantasea and about 30 minutes from Phuket International Airport. The resort offers amazing views of the Andaman Sea and features direct beach access, an all-day dining restaurant & roof top bar overlooking the sea, lobby bar, swim up pool bar, swimming pool, In-Balance Spa & Fitness, Kids Club, Free Parking, WiFi, complimentary shuttle service to Patong and modern conference & meeting spaces.
