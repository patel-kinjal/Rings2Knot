The Maru Garh Resort & Spa

Location Jodhpur, Rajasthan
Category Luxury Wedding
Standing as an oasis of natural peace and hospitality at the foothill of a gigantic sedimentary hill, combining modern architecture with the domes and arches of a bygone era, is Maru Garh.

Hidden in rustic and green setting are unique cottages, each depicting a chapter from the Great Indian Civilization.
