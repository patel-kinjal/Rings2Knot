Award-winning AYANA Resort and Spa, BALI is a world–class destination resort located on 90 hectares of cliff-top land perched above Jimbaran Bay, just 10 kilometres from Bali airport. AYANA’s majestic sunsets and ocean-front views are complemented by a white-sand beach and shared facilities as Bali’s only integrated resort with RIMBA Jimbaran BALI. With full access to all dining and recreation at both AYANA and RIMBA and complimentary resort shuttle, guests enjoy the most extensive on-site facilities of any resort in Bali.

There are 19 restaurants and bars including the iconic Rock Bar; exclusive private beach and beach club; 12 swimming pools; world-class spa with Aquatonic Seawater Therapy Pool and Spa on the Rocks treatment villas; golf-putting.
