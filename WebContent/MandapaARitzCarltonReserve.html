Mandapa A Ritz Carlton Reserve

Location Bali
Category Luxury Wedding
With rolling hills, a gentle river and verdant rice paddies as a backdrop, Ubud offers a naturally beautiful setting for a destination wedding at Mandapa, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve. The resort’s remarkable venues highlight the area’s unique culture, nature and spirituality, while local traditions impart a very real sense of authenticity.

Bridal party preparations, the wedding ceremony and cocktail reception can be accommodated in one of the luxurious three-bedroom villas, featuring a private outdoor garden and pool. The Rice Terrace lawn is an outdoor venue where its breathtaking beauty outshines any décor. Delight your guests with the crafted menus from Executive Chef Maurizio Bombini, and yourself with ancient wellness rituals to help you relax before your big day. After exchanging vows, continue your celebration with a honeymoon at this luxuriously secluded romantic resort.
