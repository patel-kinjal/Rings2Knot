The LaLit Laxmi Vilas Palace

Location Udaipur, Rajasthan
Category Luxury Wedding
The LaLiT Laxmi Vilas Palace Udaipur is located on a hilltop amidst the Aravalli hills, overlooking the Fateh Sagar lake and is close to the city center.

The LaLiT Laxmi Vilas Palace Udaipur operates 55 rooms and suites and offers over 23,000 square feet of conference and banqueting space, including a lawn that overlooks the Fateh Sagar lake.
