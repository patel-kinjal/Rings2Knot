Set along the shores of Phuket’s Bang Tao Bay in the island’s northwest, Angsana Laguna Phuket, part of Asia’s first integrated resort, Laguna Phuket. Enjoy pristine beaches with warm white sand or laze in the more than 300 metre free-form pool around the resort.

Have a fun-filled holiday with friends, a romantic getaway with your loved one or an enjoyable time bonding with family in this laidback luxury resort with endless activities.
