Marigold Green Park

Location Hyderabad
Category Luxury Wedding
Set 2.6 km from Begumpet Airport, this sleek upscale hotel in landscaped gardens is also 12 km from the 16th-century Charminar Mosque and 7 km from Hussain Sagar Lake.

The polished modern rooms and suites feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, safes and tea and coffeemaking facilities, as well as iPod docks and minibars. Upgraded rooms add complimentary cocktails and airport transfers, and suites add separate living rooms.
