The Royal Santrian

Location Bali
Category Luxury Wedding
The Royal Santrian Resort & Villa hotel in Nusa Dua is an elegant haven by the sea, a collection of luxury villas designed to fulfill your desires for seclusion, comfort and the good life.

The hotel villa resort's 2.5 hectares of lush tropical gardens slope gently down to a sparkling white sand beach of Nusa Dua. In these verdant and peaceful surroundings, you are invited to indulge in a taste of truly royal Balinese hospitality.
