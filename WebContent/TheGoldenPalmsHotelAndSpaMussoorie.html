The Golden Palms Hotel & Spa, Sylverton is a 4 star luxury hotel and its build compliments the picturesque scenic beauty. The hotel is a perfect blend of modern luxuries and old world colonial charm, reflecting the regal graciousness of the colonial era.

Live it up in style in any of the 45 well - appointed rooms and suite. The hotel well equipped for Business Travellers, Corporate Events and Family holidays. It provides a range of facilities to its guests including specialized restaurants and bars; a discotheque with Laser & Chaser lights, Business Centre, Conference Halls, Health Club and recreational facilities.

The hotel is located in close proximity to the Mall, Kulri Market. One can just spend hours watching in blissful delight the effects of the sky and an amazing view of the valley. Enjoy the surroundings within the premises which have beautifully landscaped terrace gardens. For all our guests ensuring a stay that is intimate, blissful and filled with tranquillity.
