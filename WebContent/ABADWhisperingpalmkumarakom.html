Location kumarakom, Kerala

Nestled in the scenic beauty of Kumarakom is the exotic resort destination, ABAD Whispering Palms. Set in a natural backwater setting, it is a place for experiencing the luxury of tranquillity with 53 elegantly designed garden and lake facing suites and cottages and exquisite bamboo villas with plunge pools.

You can also indulge yourself in massages from the green leaf certified Ayurvedic centers, swimming pool, business and meeting centers and a delicious range of food from our multi cuisine kitchen. There are a lot of inhouse activities for kids and thus ideal for family holidays.
