Itinerary

Day 1: Madrid: Welcome to the capital of Spain! Upon arrival, transfer to your hotel. The day is free to relax. In the evening, enjoy a great traditional flamenco show at “Café De Chinitas”. Immerse yourself in the rhythms and traditions of flamenco, during this performance at Madrid’s renowned Café de Chintas venue. Over the years this celebrated ‘tablao’ (flamenco venue) has hosted some of the country’s most famous singers and performers. Your flamenco evening also includes a drink. Overnight in Madrid.

Day 2: Madrid: After breakfast, today morning proceed for city sightseeing of Madrid and continue with the visit of the Royal Palace. Madrid shows the multiple contrasts inherited through the History. The Royal Palace was built in the XVIII century upon the ancient Baroque Italian style fortress of the Habsburg. It is one of the most beautiful and best preserved palaces in Europe and a symbol of the permanence and consolidation of Madrid as the capital of the Kingdom. Afternoon free to relax. Overnight in Madrid. (B)

Day 3: Madrid – Valencia: After breakfast, make your way to Railway Station and board your train from Madrid to Valencia. Arrive in Valencia make your way to the hotel, check in and relax. In the evening enjoy Valencia when the night falls riding a Segway (adult only). Discover the myths and legends that surround the city. Overnight in Valencia. (B)

Day 4: Valencia – Barcelona: After breakfast, make your way to Valencia Railway Station and board your train from Valencia to Barcelona. Arrive in Barcelona make your way to the hotel, check in and rest of the free to explore the city. Overnight in Barcelona. (B)

Day 5: Barcelona: After breakfast, proceed for a morning highlights tour. In Barcelona, history and traditions go hand in hand with emerging fashion and new trends in cuisine, culture and art. City offers the possibility to enjoy the sea as well as the mountains. Enjoy the most emblematic sights the city has to offer. Start with a sightseeing tour from that will take you up the elegant Passeig de Gràcia, where you will see the most famous Gaudi houses, casa Batlló and La casa Mila (La Pedrera) as well as other majestic buildings of the Modernism era. Rest of the day free for relax. Overnight in Barcelona. (B)

Day 6: Barcelona: Breakfast at the hotel. Rest of the day is free till proceed for afternoon Artistic Barcelona The Best of Gaudi, Barcelona is known as the capital of Modernism and the place where the famous architect Antoni Gaudí worked and lived. Gaudi, one of the greatest innovators of his time, has left behind numerous treasures for the discerning tourist to discover in Barcelona. This Artistic Tour of Barcelona will take you to the Sagrada Familia (Holy Family) Basilica, Gaudi’s unfinished masterpiece which is Barcelona’s most famous landmark. The walking tour of the exterior of the Sagrada Familia, to admire the richly and symbolically decorated facades. Next you will discover Gaudi’s bizarre Park Güell which has been declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Tour continues to Passeig de Gràcia to see Gaudi’s famous apartment buildings like Casa Mila “La Pedrera”, Casa Batlló and others modernist buildings. The tour ends near the Casa Batlló – Passeig de Gràcia, 43. Overnight in Barcelona. (B)

Day 7: Barcelona: After breakfast, depart to the airport, to board your flight back home OR extend your stay to Ibiza Island. (B)
